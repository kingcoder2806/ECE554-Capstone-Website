Snake n' Shake

Slithering through the hardware, one clock cycle at a time.

Project Summary

Snake n' Shake is a hardware-accelerated implementation of the classic Snake game, designed and built as part of the ECE554 capstone project. Our team set out to push the limits of what a simple game can look like when paired with custom FPGA logic, a purpose-built hardware pipeline, and a healthy dose of caffeine. From collision detection to real-time rendering, every frame is processed through carefully crafted digital circuits — no software shortcuts allowed. Whether you're dodging walls or chasing high scores, Snake n' Shake delivers the full classic experience, powered entirely by hardware.
